# QuillHaven Product Overview

QuillHaven is an AI-powered writing platform designed specifically for long-form, chapter-based storytelling. The platform serves novelists, memoir writers, and creative writers who need context-aware AI assistance for extended narratives.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Context-Aware AI**: Maintains narrative coherence across full-length novels with character, plot, and world-building consistency
- **Chapter-Based Organization**: Project management system designed for long-form writing with chapter reordering and progress tracking
- **Specialized for Creative Writing**: Unlike general AI writing tools, QuillHaven focuses on creative storytelling needs

## Target Users
- Aspiring novelists working on their first book
- Experienced genre writers managing multiple series
- Creative writing students developing thesis projects
- Indie author entrepreneurs scaling content production
- Returning writers reconnecting with creative writing

## Key Features
- AI chapter generation (2,000-5,000 words) using Gemini AI
- Character database with relationship tracking
- Plot thread monitoring across chapters
- World-building consistency maintenance
- Export to multiple formats (DOCX, PDF, TXT, EPUB)
- Version control and editing history

## Business Model
Freemium model with tiered pricing:
- **Free**: 5 chapters/month, 3 projects, basic features
- **Premium ($19.99/month)**: 25 chapters/month, unlimited projects
- **Professional ($39.99/month)**: 100 chapters/month, advanced AI features
